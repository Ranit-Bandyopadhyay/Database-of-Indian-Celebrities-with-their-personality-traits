Extroversion (E) vs Introversion (I): Extroverts are more energized around people, Introverts are energized alone

Sensing (S) vs Intuition (N): Sensing pays more attention to physical reality, Intuition pays attention to meanings and patterns of the information they get

Thinking (T) vs Feeling (F): Thinking makes decisions with their head, Feeling makes decisions with their heart

Judging (J) vs Perceiving (P): Judging is more planned and organized, Perceiving are more spontaneous.
